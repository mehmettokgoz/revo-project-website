Counting votes is a critical duty in elections, and it is subject to some problems. News agencies could count differently and the way they count is not technologically improved. Moreover, the displaying of the results depends on only one system, and this system could collapse. Currently in-use systems mostly belong to sides of elections, and this is not desired for credibility.
